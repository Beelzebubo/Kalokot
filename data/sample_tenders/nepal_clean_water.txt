================================================================================
SECTION 1: PROJECT BACKGROUND
================================================================================

The Godawari Rural Water Supply Scheme aims to provide safe drinking water
to approximately 2,500 households in Godawari Municipality, Ward 6, 7, and 8.
The project is funded by the Government of Nepal under the Ministry of
Water Supply's Universal Access to Safe Drinking Water Programme.

================================================================================
SECTION 2: SCOPE OF WORK
================================================================================

2.1 Construction of 2 intake structures at Godawari Khola
2.2 Water treatment plant (slow sand filtration, 500,000 L/day capacity)
2.3 Clear water reservoir (250,000 L capacity, RCC)
2.4 Distribution network: 12 km of HDPE/GI pipes (50mm to 150mm dia.)
2.5 Public stand posts (25 nos.) and yard connections (2,000 nos.)
2.6 Chlorination system (automatic dosing)
2.7 Testing and commissioning

Technical Specifications:
All pipes, fittings, and equipment shall conform to Nepal Standards (NS)
or equivalent international standards (ISO, ASTM, BSI). Alternative
equivalent manufacturers are acceptable subject to technical approval.
