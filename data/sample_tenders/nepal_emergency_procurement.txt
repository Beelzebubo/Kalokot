NOTICE OF EMERGENCY PROCUREMENT
(Under Section 56 of the Public Procurement Act 2063)
Ref.: DoE/EM/SF/2082-83/042
Date: 2082-03-28 (2025-07-11)

Project: Supply and delivery of classroom furniture for 120 community schools
         in Province 2 (Parsa, Bara, Rautahat districts)
Estimated Total Value: NPR 185,000,000.00
Submission Deadline: 2082-04-02 (2025-07-16) — 5 days from notice

================================================================================
JUSTIFICATION FOR EMERGENCY PROCUREMENT
================================================================================

Pursuant to Section 56 of the Public Procurement Act 2063, emergency
procurement is invoked on the following grounds:

"The start of the academic session 2082 is delayed due to furniture
shortage in 120 community schools. Regular procurement would cause
further delay of 4-5 months, disrupting the education of approximately
35,000 students. Immediate procurement is essential to prevent academic
loss."

================================================================================
SECTION 1: SCOPE OF SUPPLY
================================================================================

1.1 Two-seater student desks (wooden): 12,000 units
1.2 Teacher tables (wooden): 240 units
1.3 Teacher chairs (wooden): 240 units
1.4 Whiteboards (6'x4'): 240 units
1.5 Notice boards (4'x3'): 120 units

Total schools: 120 (see Annex A for school list)
Delivery timeline: All items to be delivered within 30 days of contract signing

================================================================================
SECTION 2: TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

2.1 Student desk: 120cm x 45cm x 76cm, Sal wood (Shorea robusta),
    finished with melamine coating. Must conform to Nepal Standard
    NS 487:2078.
2.2 Teacher table: 150cm x 75cm x 76cm, Sal wood.
2.3 Teacher chair: Standard office chair, Sal wood, cushioned seat.

Note: Sal wood furniture manufactured by Janakpur Woodcraft Industries
(P.) Ltd. is pre-approved as meeting these specifications. Equivalent
products from other manufacturers will require pre-qualification testing
overseen by the Department's Quality Assurance Section.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

3.1 Registered with Government of Nepal (Company Registrar)
3.2 Manufacturing facility capable of producing minimum 500 desks per day
3.3 Minimum 3 years experience in school furniture supply
3.4 Minimum 2 contracts of NPR 40 million each in the last 3 years

================================================================================
SECTION 4: EVALUATION
================================================================================

Due to the emergency nature of this procurement, evaluation will follow
the following simplified procedure:

4.1 Submission of sealed quotation (technical + financial)
4.2 Technical evaluation on pass/fail basis
4.3 Financial evaluation: lowest price among technically qualified bidders
4.4 No weight-based QCBS evaluation
4.5 Negotiation allowed with lowest bidder
