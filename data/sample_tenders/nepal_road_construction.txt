         (Km 22+500 to Km 35+200)

Bid Submission Deadline: 2082-02-02 (2025-05-15) — 17 days from publication

================================================================================
SECTION 1: PROJECT BACKGROUND
================================================================================

The Bhotekoshi-Sindhuli road section is a strategic link connecting the
B.P. Koirala Highway to the Chinese border trade route at Kodari. The road
is currently a gravel track and requires full upgrading to bituminous
standard. The project is funded under the National Priority Infrastructure
Development Programme (NPIDP).

================================================================================
SECTION 2: SCOPE OF WORK
================================================================================

2.1 Earthwork and subgrade preparation (12.7 km)
2.2 Base course: 200mm graded aggregate
2.3 Wearing course: 50mm bituminous concrete (asphalt)
2.4 Construction of 3 reinforced concrete bridges:
    - Bridge A: 45m span at Km 24+800
    - Bridge B: 30m span at Km 28+100
    - Bridge C: 55m span at Km 31+600
2.5 Culverts: 18 pipe culverts (900mm dia.), 4 box culverts
2.6 Road drainage: lined side drains (both sides)
2.7 Road furniture: guardrails, signage, road markings, cat's eyes
2.8 Slope protection: bio-engineering on cut slopes

Technical Specifications Note:
The asphalt wearing course shall use penetration grade 60/70 bitumen
manufactured exclusively by NepalBit Industries, Hetauda, conforming
to NS 123:2075 with batch certification from the manufacturer's own
laboratory. No alternative equivalent grades or manufacturers will
be accepted.

SECTION 4: EVALUATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

4.1 Evaluation will follow Quality and Cost Based Selection (QCBS) method.

4.2 Weights:
    - Technical Score: 30%
    - Financial Score: 70%

4.3 Technical evaluation will consider:
    - Relevant experience (15%)
    - Key personnel qualifications (10%)
    - Construction methodology (5%)

4.4 Minimum technical qualifying score: 70 out of 100

4.5 Financial evaluation: Lowest evaluated substantially responsive bid
    will receive maximum financial score. Other bids scored proportionally.

4.6 The procuring entity reserves the right to accept or reject any or
    all bids without assigning any reason.
